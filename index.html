Supporting Tomorrow's Champions

Helping talented young athletes under 15 in India who compete in State-level or National-level competitions but cannot afford essential sports equipment, training gear, and competition related expenses.

Our Mission

Every child deserves the opportunity to pursue their dreams, regardless of financial circumstances. The Manish Parmar Foundation was created to identify and support promising young athletes from economically challenged families and help them continue their sporting journey.

We are starting in Navsari, Gujarat and aim to expand across India. One athlete at a time, one dream at a time.

About the Founder

Founded by Manish Parmar, an Ontario-based community volunteer originally from Navsari, Gujarat, committed to helping talented young athletes pursue their sporting dreams.

"As someone who understands the challenges many families face, I wanted to create a simple community initiative to help talented young athletes continue their sporting journey when financial barriers stand in their way."

Manish Parmar, Founder

How to Apply

1

Submit athlete information including name, age, sport, and competition history.

2

Provide proof of State or National level participation.

3

Provide documentation demonstrating financial need.

4

Provide supporting documents from a coach, school, academy, or sports association.

5

Applications are reviewed individually. Only applicants selected for assistance may be contacted.
